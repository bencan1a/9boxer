## Quick Tips

!!! tip "Make Your First Change"
    Try dragging an employee to a different box. Click and hold the tile, drag to a new box, and release.
    The tile turns yellow to show it's been modified!

!!! warning "Remember to Save!"
    9Boxer doesn't auto-save. Click the **Apply** button when you're ready to export your changes to Excel.

!!! info "Explore Safely"
    You can't break anything! Drag employees around, click buttons, and explore.
    Nothing is saved until you click Apply and export.

## Need Help?

### Common First-Time Questions

**Q: Why is my grid empty after uploading?**
A: Check if filters are active (orange dot on Filters button). [Clear all filters](filters.md#clearing-filters)

**Q: My file won't upload. Why?**
A: Make sure column names match exactly: `Employee ID`, `Worker`, `Performance`, `Potential` (case-sensitive)
[See full upload requirements](uploading-data.md)

**Q: Can I use a CSV file?**
A: No, only Excel files (`.xlsx` or `.xls`). Convert your CSV to Excel first.

**Q: How do I save my changes?**
A: Click the **Apply** button in the top-right. This exports an updated Excel file. [Learn about exporting](exporting.md)
